AASHA MedEase FAQ's

What is AASHA MedEase?
AASHA MedEase is an AI-powered platform designed to reduce patient waiting times in hospitals by optimizing appointment scheduling and streamlining medical processes.
How does AASHA MedEase help in reducing hospital wait times?
AASHA MedEase uses AI algorithms to analyze patient data, predict peak hours, and schedule appointments efficiently, ensuring minimal waiting time for patients.
Who can use AASHA MedEase?
Hospitals, clinics, and healthcare providers can integrate AASHA MedEase to improve their patient flow. Patients can also use it to book appointments and receive real-time updates.
Is AASHA MedEase safe and reliable?
Yes, AASHA MedEase is built with secure data management and AI-driven accuracy to ensure patient information is protected and appointments are scheduled efficiently.
How can I register on AASHA MedEase?
You can register on the AASHA MedEase platform through the official website by providing your basic details and medical history.
What documents do I need for booking an appointment through AASHA MedEase?
Depending on the hospital, you may need an Aadhaar card, hospital registration number, or previous medical records for verification.
Can I book an appointment for someone else using AASHA MedEase?
Yes, you can book an appointment for family members or friends by entering their details while scheduling through the platform.
Does AASHA MedEase provide real-time appointment updates?
Yes, AASHA MedEase sends real-time updates and notifications about appointment status, estimated waiting time, and any changes to the schedule.
Is AASHA MedEase available in all hospitals?
AASHA MedEase is currently being integrated into selected hospitals. You can check the availability on our official website or app.
